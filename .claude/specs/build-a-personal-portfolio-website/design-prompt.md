# Google Stitch Design Prompt

Use this prompt in Google Stitch to generate the portfolio website layout. Two versions provided — one for each color palette option.

---

## Option A: Blue Palette

Design a single-page portfolio website for a freelance digital solutions developer who helps clubs and small local businesses. The design should feel futuristic and technology-inspired, but clean, lightweight, and bright — not dark or heavy.

**Visual style:**
- Light, airy color scheme: white/off-white backgrounds (#FAFBFE), soft blue accents (#5B9BD5), gentle teal secondary (#6BCCC8)
- Soft gradients and glowing accent areas, never harsh
- Clean modern sans-serif typography (Inter or similar) with generous whitespace
- Rounded corners (12px radius) on cards and containers
- Subtle floating geometric shapes (circles, hexagons, soft-edged triangles) as decorative elements in the hero area — these should feel like they gently float
- Overall feel: enlightening, professional, forward-looking, welcoming

**Sections (in order, top to bottom):**

1. **Navigation bar** — Sticky top bar with logo/name on the left, section links on the right (About, Mission, Projects, Testimonials, Contact). Clean, minimal. Collapses to hamburger on mobile.

2. **Hero section** — Full viewport height. Large mission statement text: "You started your business to do what you love — not to spend hours on administration, scheduling, and paperwork. I build digital solutions that take the boring stuff off your plate." Subtitle underneath. Two CTA buttons: "View My Work" and "Get In Touch". Background has a subtle animated gradient feel with 2-3 small floating geometric decorative shapes.

3. **About Me** — Two-column layout: circular photo on the left, bio text on the right. Headline "About Me". Approachable, personal tone. Background slightly different shade (#F0F4FA) to create visual separation.

4. **Mission & Values** — Section headline "Mission & Values". Brief mission paragraph. Below it, a grid of 6 value cards (2 rows x 3 columns on desktop, stacking on mobile). Each card has an icon, a value name (e.g., Reliability, Honesty, Transparency, Quality, Accessibility, Independence), and a one-line description. Cards have a subtle hover lift effect.

5. **Projects** — Section headline "My Work". Grid of 3 project cards (3 columns on desktop, stacking on mobile). Each card has: a large featured image at the top, project title, a tag/badge showing "Club" or "Firm", and three short sections labeled "Problem", "Solution", "Result". A "View Project" link at the bottom of each card. Cards have rounded corners and subtle shadow.

6. **Testimonials** — Section headline "What Clients Say". 3 testimonial cards in a row (stacking on mobile). Each card has: a large opening quote mark, the testimonial text, then the client name, role, and organization below. Optional circular client photo. Background slightly different shade for visual separation.

7. **Contact** — Section headline "Let's Talk". Brief inviting text. Contact form on the left (Name, Email, Message fields + Send button). Social links on the right (LinkedIn, GitHub icons). Clean, spacious layout.

8. **Footer** — Minimal footer with copyright, navigation links, and social icons. Dark text on light background.

**Responsive:** Mobile-first, all grids stack to single column on small screens. Navigation becomes hamburger menu.
